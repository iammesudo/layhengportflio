About Me

I'm Loem Layheng — an 18-year-old tech-driven professional from Phnom Penh, Cambodia. I'm currently pursuing a dual degree in Management and English while working as a Junior Product Owner at Youding, a POS software company.

"I've often been judged as too young or inexperienced. In reality, my curiosity and willingness to learn have enabled me to do things others hesitate to try."

My approach is defined by flexibility and an insatiable hunger for learning. I don't just use technology — I study how it can optimise businesses. I prefer starting with action rather than long planning phases. Real issues show up during execution, and I fix and improve along the way.

My goal is to help Cambodian businesses adopt practical technology solutions that reduce manual work and improve efficiency. I specialise in software-driven projects — data management systems, centralised platforms, and internal tools.
