Junior Product Owner

EZorder F&B 2.0 Webapp

A mobile web application that transforms the dine-in experience — customers scan a QR code and handle everything from browsing menus to completing payment, right from their phones.

Role
Junior Product Owner
Type
Mobile Web App
Industry
F&B / Restaurant
Company
Youding
Period
Dec 2025 – Feb 2026
The Problem
"Restaurants were losing efficiency — staff constantly moving between tables just to take orders and process payments, leading to delays and poor customer experience."

EZorder 2.0 was designed to solve this entirely. By giving customers a direct channel via QR code, the product eliminates the dependency on staff for routine order-and-pay interactions.

My Contribution

As Junior Product Owner, I worked across the full product lifecycle — from defining user stories and wireframe feedback to coordinating with the dev team on sprint priorities and release readiness.

01
User story mapping — defined customer journeys from QR scan to payment completion
02
Feature prioritisation — worked with stakeholders to scope MVP vs. backlog
03
UAT coordination — organised testing rounds with real restaurant clients and captured feedback
04
Documentation — wrote product specs, flow diagrams, and handoff notes for dev team
Outcome

The app successfully reduced table service time and allowed restaurant staff to focus on food quality and hospitality rather than order-taking. Deployed with live restaurant clients under the Youding platform.
